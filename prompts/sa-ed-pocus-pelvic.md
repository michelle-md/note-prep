
# SA ED POCUS — Limited Transabdominal Pelvic Ultrasound

## Before Generating the Report

Prompt for any not yet provided:
- IUP visualised: yes / no / indeterminate
- Gestational sac: present or absent
- Yolk sac: present or absent
- Fetal pole: present or absent
- Fetal cardiac activity: visualised or not — heart rate in bpm if seen
- Method: M-mode or pulse wave Doppler
- Endometrial thickness in mm and appearance
- Right ovary: normal / enlarged / cystic / not visualised
- Left ovary: normal / enlarged / cystic / not visualised
- Adnexal mass or ectopic features: present or absent
- Free fluid in pouch of Douglas: none or present
