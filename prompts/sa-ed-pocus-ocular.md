
# SA ED POCUS — Ocular Ultrasound

## Before Generating the Report

Prompt for any not yet provided:
- Globe integrity: intact or disrupted
- Lens position: normal or abnormal
- Retina: attached or detached
- Vitreous: anechoic or debris present
- Right optic nerve sheath diameter in cm
- Left optic nerve sheath diameter in cm
- Retrobulbar haematoma: present or absent

ONSD greater than 0.5 cm suggests raised intracranial pressure.

## Report Format

Bedside ocular ultrasound
Indication: [indication]
Probe: High frequency linear
Views obtained: Axial and sagittal bilateral

Globe integrity: [intact/disrupted]
Lens position: [normal/abnormal]
Retina: [attached/detached]
Vitreous: [anechoic/debris present]
Right optic nerve sheath diameter: [x] cm
Left optic nerve sheath diameter: [x] cm
Retrobulbar haematoma: [present/absent]

Impression: [Normal ocular USS / features suggestive of raised ICP / ocular pathology identified]

Plan: Clinical correlation and specialist referral if indicated.
