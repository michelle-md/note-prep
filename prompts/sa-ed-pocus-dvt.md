
# SA ED POCUS — Lower Limb DVT Ultrasound

## Before Generating the Report

Prompt for any not yet provided:
- Common femoral vein: compressible or non-compressible
- Deep femoral vein: compressible or non-compressible
- Popliteal vein: compressible or non-compressible
- Trifurcation: compressible or non-compressible
- Colour Doppler flow: normal or abnormal
- Side examined: right / left / bilateral

## Report Format

Bedside lower limb DVT ultrasound
Indication: Assessment for deep vein thrombosis
Probe: Linear
Technique: 3-point compression with colour Doppler
Side examined: [right/left/bilateral]

Common femoral vein: [compressible/non-compressible]
Deep femoral vein: [compressible/non-compressible]
Popliteal vein: [compressible/non-compressible]
Trifurcation: [compressible/non-compressible]
Colour Doppler flow: [normal/abnormal]

Impression: [No sonographic evidence of proximal DVT / findings consistent with proximal DVT]

Plan: Clinical correlation and formal duplex ultrasound if indicated.
