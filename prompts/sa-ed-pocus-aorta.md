
# SA ED POCUS — Abdominal Aortic Ultrasound

## Before Generating the Report

Prompt for any not yet provided:
- Aortic diameter above celiac trunk in cm
- Aortic diameter below celiac trunk in cm
- Aortic diameter proximal to bifurcation in cm
- Dissection flap: present or absent
- Mural thrombus: present or absent
- Free fluid: present or absent

Normal aortic diameter is less than 3.0 cm — flag if greater than or equal to 3.0 cm.

## Report Format

Bedside abdominal aortic ultrasound
Indication: [indication]
Probe: Curvilinear
Views obtained: Longitudinal and transverse from celiac trunk to bifurcation

Above celiac trunk: [x] cm
Below celiac trunk: [x] cm
Proximal to bifurcation: [x] cm
Dissection flap: [present/absent]
Mural thrombus: [present/absent]
Free fluid: [present/absent]

Impression: [No evidence of aneurysm or dissection / evidence of AAA / dissection flap identified]

Plan: Clinical correlation and formal imaging if indicated.
