
# SA ED POCUS — Gallbladder Ultrasound

## Before Generating the Report

Prompt for any not yet provided:
- Gallbladder wall thickness in cm
- Gallstones: present or absent
- Sludge: present or absent
- Pericholecystic fluid: present or absent
- Gallbladder wall hyperaemia: present or absent
- Common bile duct diameter in cm
- Sonographic Murphy sign: positive or negative

## Report Format

Bedside gallbladder ultrasound
Indication: [indication]
Probe: Curvilinear
Views obtained: Longitudinal and transverse gallbladder

Gallbladder wall thickness: [x] cm
Gallstones: [present/absent]
Sludge: [present/absent]
Pericholecystic fluid: [present/absent]
Gallbladder wall hyperaemia: [present/absent]
Common bile duct diameter: [x] cm
Sonographic Murphy sign: [positive/negative]

Impression: [Findings consistent/not consistent with cholecystitis or biliary pathology]

Plan: Clinical correlation and formal imaging if indicated.
