
# SA ED Billing Codes

Clinician confirmation required before all code submissions.

## Base Code

0190 — Emergency consultation — required for all ED encounters.

## Procedure Codes

7063 — Resuscitation / complex emergency procedure
Required documentation: specific interventions performed.
Examples: IV resuscitation, dual antiplatelet loading, thrombolysis,
cardioversion, intubation, chest drain, central line, arterial line,
procedural sedation, primary PCI activation.

7064 — Critical care / monitoring
Required documentation: time-based, continuous monitoring, critical care.
Examples: haemodynamic monitoring, ventilator management, vasopressor initiation.

7063 vs 7064:
7063: procedural complexity drives the claim.
7064: time and monitoring intensity drives the claim.
Both may be claimed if both criteria independently met and documented.
Clinician must confirm which applies and document the basis.

1230 — ECG interpretation — submit when ECG performed and interpreted. Medical patients only.

## Modifier Codes

Apply after-hours, weekend, and public holiday modifiers where applicable.
